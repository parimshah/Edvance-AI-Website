Our Story

How We Started
↓

As students, researchers, and engineers we've been part of the AI revolution over the past several years, like so many others. We've witnessed its impact on our lives and its potential to reshape the world. Hoping to ensure that the next generation is empowered to harness this powerful technology for good in this world, we founded Edvance AI. As an international non-profit, we're working tirelessly to host free, high-quality AI workshops, provide in-depth resources, and establish a mentorship program for student-led AI projects that are harnessing AI for good. We're expanding from coast to coast and into numerous school districts worldwide, aiming to reach students of all backgrounds, interests, and skill levels.
